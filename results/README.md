# Results

Derived artifacts only. No raw MIMIC data; no row-level PHI. Curated outputs that the manuscript cites are checked in here once the pipeline runs. Intermediate per-record per-event tables stay gitignored under `data/interim/` and `results/raw/`.

## Reproducibility manifest

`results/manifest.json` will list, for each canonical artifact, its path, SHA-256, byte size, and the script and seed that produced it. Generated by `scripts/31_write_manifest.py` (port from sibling).
